FAQ (from ISEFY App & device settings):
1. Can I set detection zones for the two lenses on DualWatch separately?
   Yes. In Detection Zone settings, you can switch between the two lenses and set different detection areas for each view.
2. How do I set up SD card for continuous recording?
   Go to Device Details → Local Storage Settings. Turn on Scheduled Recording to enable continuous recording.
3. Which ONVIF profiles do ISEFY cameras support?
   ISEFY cameras support ONVIF Profile S, which covers basic video streaming and device discovery.
4. Can I view video without internet?
   Yes. Double‑click the reset button to activate Soft AP mode, connect your phone to the device hotspot, then use Tool → LAN Live in the ISEFY app to view locally.
5. Can I use the camera without an SD card?
   Yes. You can still view the live stream and receive push notifications. Video playback and recording require either an SD card or Cloud storage.

FAQ (common ISEFY camera questions):
1. The indicator light is flashing red—what should I do?
   Reset the device. If the indicator changes to flashing green, re‑add the camera in the ISEFY App. If it stays red, the camera may be faulty—please contact ISEFY Support.
2. How do I check if the camera connection is stable?
   Check the Wi‑Fi icon in the Device Details page—green full bars indicate a good connection. You can also use the Wi‑Fi Detection Tool in the ISEFY app.
3. How do I switch to Night Vision mode?
   Ensure the device is online → Device Details → Night Vision Function → select your preferred mode.
4. Can I set a schedule for detection and alerts?
   Yes. In Detection Settings, configure detection schedules for specific days and times to receive alerts only when needed.
5. What is Privacy Mode?
   When Privacy Mode is enabled, the camera lens is physically blocked and the device will not capture video or send alerts. No images or information will be recorded during this time.

FAQ:
1. Why don’t I receive a phone call or alert when someone presses the doorbell?
   Enable notifications in the ISEFY app (Device Details → Notification). Also enable ISEFY notifications in your phone system settings.
2. Which detection mode should I use—Motion Detection or Human Detection? And what is PIR?
   We recommend Human Detection. It uses PIR (Passive Infrared Sensor), which triggers alarms by sensing heat and motion—reducing false alarms effectively.
3. Does Pivot Doorbell support 5GHz Wi‑Fi?
   Yes. Pivot is dual‑band and supports both 2.4GHz and 5GHz Wi‑Fi.

5. Do I need an angled bracket to install Pivot?
